## Cross-Cutting Analysis Examples

The power of separating Area from Dimension:

**"Speed is our worst dimension"**
- F&B: slow-kitchen, long-queue
- Front Desk: slow-checkin, long-wait
- Parking & Transport: late-shuttle, slow-transportation
→ Systemic staffing or process issue, not a departmental one

**"Cleanliness varies by area"**
- Room: clean-property (positive)
- Bathroom: moldy-bathroom (negative)
- Pool: dirty-pool (negative)
→ Housekeeping prioritizes rooms over wet areas

**"Condition problems concentrated in one building"**
- Room: broken-ac, peeling-paint
- Bathroom: leaky-faucet, clogged-drain
- Facilities: broken-elevator
→ Capital expenditure needed for specific building/wing
